About

AI Therapist offering personalized therapy for employees & AI scanner to monitor org level mental health.

Enterprises face hidden costs from poor employee mental health, including reduced productivity, increased sick leaves, and low motivation. Traditional methods often fail to provide personalized support, leading to low utilization (under 5%) and poor ROI. In-house or contracted therapists also come with friction like wait times, societal stigma, and patient-therapist fit issues.

We are building a personalized AI mental coach and therapist. It offers evidence-based, personalized counseling and therapy. Additionally, our platform includes an employer dashboard to track company-wide mental health metrics while ensuring the confidentiality of employee-AI coach conversations.
