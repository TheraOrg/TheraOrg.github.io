About

Comprehensive Mental Wellness Package for Your Organization

Organizations face hidden costs from poor employee mental health, such as reduced productivity, increased sick leave, and low motivation. Existing Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs) lack personalized support and feedback to the employers, leading to underutilization (less than 5%) and poorly informed policies. In-house or contracted therapists can also face challenges such as long wait times, societal stigma, and issues with patient-therapist fit.

TheraOrg offers a two-fold solution to this problem:

- Personalized coaching, counseling, and psychotherapy to enhance employee well-being.

- An employer dashboard that provides detailed insights, monitors company-wide mental health trends, and facilitates the design of organization-wide interventions (e.g., positive leadership, conflict resolution, change management), all while ensuring complete confidentiality of employee-AI interactions.
